Our Roots

Born in
Sri Lanka

Founded in the serene hills of Kandy, Bakes By Blunch (BBB) started with a singular, clean vision: to combine modern baking professionalism with uncompromising Sri Lankan heritage.

We discarded the artificial colors and heavy fondants. Instead, we use only the finest natural ingredients—creamy butter, premium Ceylon cocoa, and aromatic spices. Our approach is minimal, our execution is precise, and our taste is unequivocally authentic.
